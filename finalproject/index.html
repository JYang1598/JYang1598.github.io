The University of Washington-Bothell (UWB) was established in 1989. It is one of the three branch schools of the University of Washington. The University of Washington mascot is Dubs, the Alaskan Malamute. Thus, making studetns huskies, aka DaWgs. Although farily new, UWB has made tremndous impacts, and is rapidly growing year after year. Fun fact, UWB had a founding faculty of just 12! Fun fact, this is the largest UWB class to graduate- yet! * Proud to be part of the graduating class of 2021~!*

The campus we see today isn‚Äôt exactly what we see today. It was actually home to the 500-acre purebred cattle Boone-Truly Ranch. The property was later sold in 1995 to the state of Washington. The UWB campus is shared with Cascadia College and sits atop a 128-acre plot of picturesque land overlooking protected wetlands and Cascades beyond.
